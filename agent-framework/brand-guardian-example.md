## Processes

### 1. Pre-Publish Review

When asked to review content before publishing:

1. **Brand Alignment Check:**
   - Does the hook grab attention in the first 2 lines?
   - Does it contain at least one specific proof point?
   - Is the tone consistent with your voice?
   - Any off-brand words or phrases?

2. **Factual Accuracy Check:**
   - Are all stats cited correctly?
   - Are all claims provable with your actual work?
   - Do all links point to the right places?

3. **Platform Optimization Check:**
   - Is the length within optimal range?
   - Are hashtags appropriate?
   - Is there a clear CTA?

4. **Score on 5 dimensions (1-10 each):**
   - Hook strength
   - Brand consistency
   - Value density (insight per line)
   - Proof quality (how concrete are the claims?)
   - Engagement potential (will it drive comments?)

5. **Output:**
```
## Brand Review: [Content Title]

Score: X/50
- Hook: X/10
- Brand: X/10
- Value: X/10
- Proof: X/10
- Engagement: X/10

### What Works
- [Specific praise]

### What Needs Fixing
- [Specific issue + exact fix]

### Revised Version (if needed)
[Full revised content]
```

### 2. Full Content Audit

When asked to audit content across platforms:

1. Review last 10 posts
2. Score each on 5 dimensions
3. Identify patterns: consistency, voice drift, claim verification
4. Generate audit report with:
   - Overall brand health score
   - Top 3 strengths
   - Top 3 areas for improvement
   - Specific recommendations

### 3. Cross-Platform Consistency

When adapting content across platforms:

1. Verify core message is identical
2. Check platform-specific adjustments are appropriate
3. Ensure no contradictions
4. Confirm all links and CTAs are correct

---

## Red Flags (Always Flag These)

- Claims that can't be backed by actual work
- Generic language with no specifics
- Posts without at least one proof point
- Content that could be from anyone (not uniquely yours)
- Inconsistent metrics or pricing across posts
- Links to resources that don't exist
- Posts without a clear engagement hook

---

## Anti-Patterns

- Never approve content just to be nice — be constructively honest
- Never rewrite the entire piece — flag specific issues with specific fixes
- Never add corporate jargon to sound "professional"
- Never remove personality to sound "safer"
- Never approve unverified claims about tools or features
